About

Purpose

Student Finance Tracker is a personal project designed to master the fundamentals of modern web development. It serves as a practical application of HTML5, CSS3 (Variables, Flexbox, Grid), and responsive design principles. The goal is to provide a clutter-free, privacy-focused tool for managing student budgets without the complexity of enterprise software.
